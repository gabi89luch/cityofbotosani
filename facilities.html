Municipal Stadium

It is a multi-functional stadium, the host of matches of the FC Botoșani team, it is administered by the Local Council through the Public Service for the Administration of Sports Bases and Leisure Areas Botoșani. The municipal has a capacity of 7,782 seats, all on seats, and an area of 31,674 m².

The stadium benefits from nighttime, inaugurated in September 2014 during a match with Dinamo Bucharest, a match won by the Botoșani 3-2.

Cornisa AquaPark & Sports

Stretched over an area of 48 ha, at the western exit of the Botoșani municipality towards Suceava, the Cornișa Regional Tourist and Sports Leisure Park is worth discovering!

Adapted to current comfort requirements, the CORNISA AquaPark & Sports universe offers you everything you need to have a perfect day of relaxation, far from the alert and tiring rhythm of the city, regardless of the season.
